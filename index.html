Welcome to Ask-AMU Togo

Ask-AMU is a virtual assistant available on major social media platforms such as WhatsApp (available), Facebook (in progress), and TikTok (in progress). It is specifically designed to communicate, educate, inform, and facilitate the active participation of the population in Universal Health Coverage (Assurance Maladie Universelle) in Togo. This conversational agent helps users to identify their health insurance plan, determine the associated managing organization, and clearly understand their financial contribution.

Features About Contact

Features

  • Identify your health insurance plan
  • Understand your financial contribution
  • Access via WhatsApp, Facebook, and TikTok
  • Support for Universal Health Coverage

About Ask-AMU

Ask-AMU Togo is a revolutionary tool designed to enhance public awareness and engagement in the Universal Health Coverage program in Togo. By leveraging popular social media platforms, Ask-AMU provides easy access to essential health insurance information, empowering citizens to make informed decisions about their healthcare.
