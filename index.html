Meet Ask-AMU: Your Smart AI-Powered Assistant

Ask-AMU is here to guide you on popular social media platforms WhatsApp, Telegram, and TikTok

Designed to inform, educate, and empower, Ask-AMU makes it easier than ever for you to engage with Universal Health Coverage (UHC) in Togo (Assurance Maladie Universelle). Whether you're figuring out your health insurance plan, identifying your managing organization, understanding your financial contribution, or pre-registrations, Ask-AMU simplifies it all.

This conversational agent helps users identify their health insurance plan, determine the associated managing organization, and clearly understand their financial contribution.
